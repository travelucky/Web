Project Purpose

The "Entertainment Paradise" project was created to provide users with a unique, interactive platform that combines games, movies, and books into a single experience. It allows users to accumulate points through gaming and use those points to unlock content such as movies and books. Our primary aim is to offer a seamless, engaging environment that promotes entertainment and community interaction.

Project Timeline

The project was conceived and started in early 2024. The initial concept was developed over a few months, followed by stages of design, development, and testing. We are now in the phase of user testing and continuous improvement.

  • January 2024: Initial project conception and planning.
  • March 2024: Design and user interface development.
  • May 2024: Backend and frontend integration completed.
  • July 2024: Testing and user feedback phase.
  • October 2024: Public launch and ongoing updates.
